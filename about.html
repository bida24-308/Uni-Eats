Our Story

Revolutionizing how people eat, one meal at a time

Our Mission

At FreshBite, we believe that healthy eating should be convenient, affordable, and delicious. We're committed to making nutritious meals accessible to everyone by combining farm-fresh ingredients with innovative meal planning and exceptional customer service.

How It Started

FreshBite was born in 2018 when our founder, Alex Chen, struggled to find time for healthy eating while building his startup. Frustrated by processed delivery meals and limited options, he partnered with local chefs and nutritionists to create a meal service that would be both nourishing and convenient.

What started as a small operation delivering to 50 customers has grown into a thriving community of over 50,000 active members. Today, FreshBite collaborates with 30+ local farms and 15 award-winning chefs to deliver exceptional meals to thousands of households every week.
